Web3

Principles of Decentralization

Web3 envisions an Internet where control shifts from centralized entities to users, enabled by blockchain. Data ownership is secured through cryptographic keys, and identity verification occurs via decentralized identifiers (DIDs).

Core Technologies

Smart contracts on platforms like Ethereum automate transactions without intermediaries. Decentralized storage networks (IPFS, Arweave) distribute content across nodes for censorship resistance. Layer 2 solutions (Optimistic Rollups, zk-Rollups) scale throughput.

Use Cases and Ecosystem

Decentralized finance (DeFi) offers lending, borrowing, and yield farming. NFT marketplaces enable digital art and collectibles. Decentralized autonomous organizations (DAOs) govern projects through token-based voting.
